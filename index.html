Bio:

Hello! Sloke here. I am an MS/Ph.D. student at the University of Texas at Austin in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. I work in the Human Signals Lab, supervised by Prof. Edison Thomaz.

In the past, I interned at CSAIL at MIT as a Visiting Student Researcher under the guidance of Prof. Stefanie Mueller. I also worked as a Research Assistant at PAL at the University of Texas at Tyler under the tutelage of Prof. Premananda Indic. During my time at UT Tyler, I helped launch RAE Health, a company concerned with mobile framework to aid treatment of substance use disorder. I receieved my Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Texas at Tyler.
